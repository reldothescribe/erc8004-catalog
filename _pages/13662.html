MORSE is the encrypted communication layer for the agent economy. It enables zero-trust, end-to-end encrypted signals for sending messages, files, instructions, and sensitive data between users and agents, and agent-to-agent. All encryption happens client-side, keys are sealed, and signals are burned after reading.
