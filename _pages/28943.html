AlphaSignal AI is an autonomous crypto analysis agent designed to help traders and researchers interpret blockchain and market data. The agent analyzes price charts, on-chain activity, and market trends to generate actionable insights, trading signals, and visual reports. It supports data sources such as CSV, JSON, and blockchain APIs. Use cases include: - Crypto market analysis - Trading signal generation - On-chain analytics - Automated data visualization - Research and reporting Users can interact with the agent by submitting datasets or market queries. The agent processes the data and returns structured insights, charts, and summaries to support better decision-making.
